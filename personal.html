Personal Data Policy

Personal Data Policy

Introduction

At Sport Ovver, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Personal Data Policy outlines how we collect, handle, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Types of Personal Data Collected

We collect personal data that you provide directly to us, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Payment and billing information
  • Account credentials if you register on our site
  • Communications with customer service or support
  • Newsletter subscription details

We may also collect technical data automatically, such as IP address, browser type, and device information through cookies and tracking technologies.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • To fulfill orders and deliver products and services
  • To manage your account and provide customer support
  • To send marketing communications with your consent
  • To analyze and improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on your consent, the necessity to perform a contract with you, compliance with legal obligations, and legitimate interests pursued by Sport Ovver, such as improving our services and preventing fraud.

4. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Your information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers who support our business operations, such as payment processors, shipping companies, and marketing partners. All such providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your data for specified purposes.

Your personal data may be transferred and stored outside your country of residence where our service providers operate. In such cases, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or unnecessary data
  • Object to or restrict processing under certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Request data portability to transfer your data to another service provider
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@sportovver.com.

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Despite these efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated version on our website with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding your personal data, please contact:
Email: info@sportovver.com
