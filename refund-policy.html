Returns & Refunds Policy

Sport Ovver operates as an affiliate partner, connecting you with trusted retailers and brands. We do not sell products directly, so all purchases are made on the retailer’s website.

1. How Returns and Refunds Work

  • Returns and refunds are handled exclusively by the retailer from whom you purchase the product.
  • If you need to return or request a refund for an order, please follow the retailer’s returns policy, which is usually available on their website or your order confirmation.
  • We recommend reviewing the retailer’s shipping, return, and refund terms before completing your purchase.

2. Our Role as an Affiliate

  • Sport Ovver provides product recommendations and links to retailers, earning a commission on sales made through our site.
  • We do not control retailer policies or shipping, so any issues with returns or refunds must be directed to the retailer’s customer service.

3. What to Do If You Have an Issue

  • For assistance with a return or refund, please contact the retailer directly using the information provided in your order confirmation email.
  • If you have questions about the product or need help finding retailer contact details, feel free to reach out to us at info@sportovver.com, and we’ll do our best to assist.

4. Important Notes

  • Because Sport Ovver does not handle orders or payments, we cannot initiate returns, issue refunds, or manage shipping on behalf of retailers.
  • Any warranty or guarantee claims must be addressed directly with the product manufacturer or retailer.

If you have any questions about shopping through Sport Ovver, please contact us at info@sportovver.com.
